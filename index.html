Regulatory Disclosures

Broker Status: Crisply is an Air Charter Broker and acts as an agent for its clients or as an indirect air carrier, depending on the specific engagement. Crisply is not a Direct Air Carrier and does not own, maintain, or operate any aircraft.

Operational Control: All flights arranged by Crisply are performed by fully FAA-licensed and DOT-registered Part 135 Direct Air Carriers (or foreign equivalent) who maintain full operational control of the aircraft at all times. These carriers are solely responsible for the air transportation services.

Consumer Protection: In accordance with 14 CFR Part 295, clients are entitled to receive the name of the operating Direct Air Carrier prior to the flight. If this information is unavailable at the time of booking, it will be provided as soon as it becomes available.
